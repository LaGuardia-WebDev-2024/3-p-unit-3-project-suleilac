Cupcake Recipe

  • Time: 40-60 minutes
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 and 1/4 cup
Baking Powder 1 and 1/4 teaspoons
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Unsalted Butter 1/2 cup
Granulated Sugar 3/4 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla Extract 2 teaspoons
Buttermilk 1/2 cup

Step 1: In a medium bowl, whisk together dry ingredients: flour, baking powder, and salt.

Step 2: In a second bowl (or bowl of your stand mixer), beat together softened butter and sugar on medium-high speed 5 minutes until thick and fluffy.

Step 3: Beat in eggs 1 at a time, mixing to incorporate with each egg then blend in vanilla and scrape down the bowl.

Step 4: Reduce mixer to medium speed and add the flour in thirds, alternating with adding the 1/2 cup buttermilk and beating well between additions.

Step 5: Pour batter into 12 lined muffin tins, filling 2/3 full. Bake in the center of the oven at 350ËšF for 20-23 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let cool 5 minutes in the pan then remove cupcakes to a wire rack to cool completely.

Vanilla Cake Recipe

  • Time: 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Serves: 24
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Baking Powder 2 and 1/2 teaspoons
Salt 1/4 teaspoons
Eggs 4
Sugar 1 and 1/2 cup
Unsalted butter 1/2 cup
Milk 1 cup
Vanilla extract 3 teaspoons
Vegetable/canola oil 3 teaspoons

Step 1: Beat the eggs with sugar for a whole 7 minutes until thick and glossy, it changes from yellow to white, and tripled in volume (just over double depth in the bowl).

Step 2: Whisk together the flour, baking powder and salt. Then add into egg mixture in 3 lots, mixing for just 5 seconds on Speed 1 in between.

Step 3: Use a microwave or stove to heat milk with the butter until it melts

Step 4: Mix some batter into hot milk.

Step 5: Slowly pour milk mixture back into whipped eggs over 20 seconds on Speed 1. Scrape down sides of bowls, then mix for 10 seconds on Speed 1.

Step 6: Pour into 2 cake pans lined with parchment/baking paper.

Step 7: Bake 30 minutes until golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out clean.

Apple Pie Recipe

  • Time: 3 hour 30 minutes
  • Serves: 12

Dough:

Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 and 1/2 cup
Sugar 4 teaspoons
Salt 1/4 teaspoon
Cold Unsalted butter 14 tablespoons
Egg 1 (lightly beaten with 2 tablespoons cold water)

Filling:

Ingredients Quantity
Fresh lemon juice 2 tablespoons
Apples 3 pounds worth
Sugar 3/4 cup
Unsalted butter 1/4 cup
Ground cinnamon 1/4 teaspoon
Ground Nutmeg A generous pinch
Egg 1 (lightly beaten)

Step 1: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt. Using your fingers, work the butter into the dry ingredients until it resembles yellow corn meal mixed with bean sized bits of butter.

Step 2: Add the egg and stir the dough together with a fork or by hand in the bowl. If the dough is dry, sprinkle up to a tablespoon more of cold water over the mixture.

Step 3: Form the dough into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate until thoroughly chilled, at least 1 hour.

Step 4: Put the lemon juice in a medium bowl. Peel, halve, and core the apples. Cut each half into 4 wedges. Toss the apple with the lemon juice. Add the sugar and toss to combine evenly.

Step 5: In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium-high heat. Add the apples, and cook, stirring, until the sugar dissolves and the mixture begins to simmer, about 2 minutes. Cover, reduce heat to medium-low, and cook until the apples soften and release most of their juices, about 7 minutes.

Step 6: Strain the apples in a colander over a medium bowl to catch all the juice. Shake the colander to get as much liquid as possible. Return the juices to the skillet, and simmer over medium heat until thickened and lightly caramelized, about 10 minutes.

Step 7: In a medium bowl, toss the apples with the reduced juice and spices. Set aside to cool completely.

Step 8: Cut the dough in half. On a lightly floured surface, roll each half of dough into a disc about 11 to 12 inches wide. Layer the dough between pieces of parchment or wax paper on a baking sheet, and refrigerate for at least 10 minutes.

Step 9: Place a rack in the lower third of the oven and preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.

Step 10: Line the bottom of a 9-inch pie pan with one of the discs of dough, and trim it so it lays about 1/2 inch beyond the edge of the pan. Put the apple filling in the pan and mound it slightly in the center. Brush the top edges of the dough with the egg. Place the second disc of dough over the top. Fold the top layer of dough under the edge of the bottom layer and press the edges together to form a seal. Flute the edge as desired. Brush the surface of the dough with egg and then sprinkle with sugar. Pierce the top of the dough in several places to allow steam to escape while baking. Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes.

Step 11: Bake the pie on a baking sheet until the crust is golden, about 50 minutes. Cool on a rack before serving.
